Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Oakland Pride 2026.

Oakland Pride takes place on Sunday, August 16, 2026.

The parade and festival take place in Downtown Oakland, centered around Frank H. Ogawa Plaza / City Hall.

The majority of Oakland Pride is all ages. Our Sexy Town area is 18+ only.

The festival requires a ticket. You can purchase online or at the event.

Visit our Participate page to learn about volunteering, performing, vending, or joining the parade.
